About us

My story begins in Angeles City, Philippines. Growing up, I’ve always been fascinated by the clean aesthetics
of Tokyo, Japan. These aesthetics are now constantly present in all of the designs of each of our cafe
locations. In addition, I was raised by parents who were avid coffee-drinkers who would teach me about the
different complexities of different coffee notes. We would often spend our summer breaks travelling to
different places – immersing ourselves in the cultures and flavors present in all of the food we would try.

Locations

Manila W City Center | Philippines

Our first store in the Philippines is located at W City Centre in the contemporary,fun and populated
Bonifacio Global City. Bonifacio Global City is the home of passionate minds where form and function
come together. High architectural structures, playful green parks, diverse street art and many leisure activities
all merge in one area, making it the place to experience modern Philippines.
Located right across the famed Bonifacio High Street, W City Center is a 29-story building offering an
unobstructed view of the Bonifacio global city skyline. It’s near a thoroughfare of the district and well within
walking distance to local transport hubs, markets, shopping districts, restaurants, offices and schools.
In all this hustle and bustle, we excitedly look forward to your visit!
